# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers configuring a triangulated background for an interface, and designers who want to explore and export a finished background without installing the library. Their primary task is to adjust appearance, lighting, and output settings quickly while seeing the result immediately.

## Product Purpose

Triangles is a seeded, renderer-switchable background generator. The playground makes its configuration tangible: users can tune a deterministic Delaunay surface, compare renderers, and export an image or SVG for use elsewhere.

## Brand Personality

Technical, composed, and inviting. The tool should feel capable without requiring specialist knowledge.

## Anti-references

Avoid a dense developer-debug panel, a generic dashboard treatment, and ornate visual decoration that competes with the generated background. Do not hide important controls behind unclear interactions.

## Design Principles

- Keep the generated surface as the visual focus.
- Group controls by the decision a user is making, not by implementation detail.
- Make advanced configuration easy to discover and safe to adjust.
- Preserve fast visual feedback for every setting.
- Let both designers and developers reach exports without needing documentation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, standard keyboard-operable controls, and clear labels. No additional accessibility accommodations are currently required.
